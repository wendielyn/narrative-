My Work Reflection

The two-week work immersion at the Junior High School Administration Office provided valuable hands-on experiences and fostered professional growth. Engaging in various tasks such as data encoding, document delivery, organizing SLAN documents, and assisting in office errands highlighted the importance of time management, accuracy, and attention to detail. Collaborating with Ma’am Alma, Ma’am Jen, and other employees offered opportunities to practice effective communication and teamwork, which were crucial in meeting daily responsibilities efficiently.

Despite challenges like the accidental deletion of files and time constraints, adaptability and persistence played key roles in completing assignments. The immersion experience not only strengthened technical skills such as file management and data organization but also instilled a strong sense of accountability and discipline. These lessons will undoubtedly serve as valuable assets in future professional endeavors.
