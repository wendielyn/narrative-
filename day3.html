Day 3: Immersion Narrative

Upon arriving at the office, I retrieved the paper Ma’am Alma gave me and collected the names of the immersion students. I then created an attendance sheet on the computer, printed it, and posted it outside the office. Later, I went to the principal’s office to deliver documents for signing and handed in leave letters for the INC teachers. After lunch, Ma’am Alma gave me extra copies to distribute to various Junior High departments. We were also tasked with transferring undergraduate papers into file holders. Once we completed the task, Ma’am Jenny asked us to buy snacks at the front gate. Afterward, I resumed encoding the names of undergraduate students in Excel.
